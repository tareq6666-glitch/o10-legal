User Data Deletion Instructions — o10

Effective Date: December 16, 2025

If you used Google Sign-In and/or Facebook Login with the o10 app and want to delete your data, follow the instructions below.

Option 1 — Request deletion directly from o10 (recommended)

Email us at o10.coffee.shop@gmail.com from the email address linked to your o10 account with the subject: “Account Deletion Request”.

In your message, include:

  • Your o10 account email
  • (Optional) Your displayed name in the app
  • (Optional) Whether you signed in using Google or Facebook

We will delete your account and associated app data (orders, wallet records, reservations, referral data, coupons, and draw participation), except where retention is required by law or for legitimate security purposes.

Option 2 — Remove o10 access from your Google or Facebook account

Removing access stops future data sharing from Google/Facebook to o10. It may not delete data already stored by o10. To delete stored app data, please also use Option 1.

A) Facebook

  1. Open Facebook Settings
  2. Go to Apps and Websites (or a similar section)
  3. Find o10 and select Remove

B) Google

  1. Open your Google Account settings
  2. Go to Security
  3. Find Third-party access (or “Your connections to third-party apps & services”)
  4. Select o10 and choose Remove Access
